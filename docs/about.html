About Typus

Typus originated as an idea formed by Ryan Tsang, and was brought to life with the collaboration of Ayman, Ryan, Jonathan and Parker. Typus is a full stack mobile application that utilizes Thunkable as a Front-End and Firebase as a Back-End. The application incorporates our web API's using Firebase Google Cloud Functions for the generation of our Leaderboard and Levenstein.
