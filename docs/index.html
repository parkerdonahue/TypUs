Have you heard of the game wordle?

Well, Typus is wordle with typing!
There are pre-existing typing games on mobile, but none of them present themselves as a simple and fun challenge that you keep coming back to like ours!

Instructions for Typus

  1. Sign up to create an account with us or you can use our optional free play button
  2. Signing in will then lead you to your very own profile page!
  3. Then, all you will have to do is click on play!
  4. A prompt will appear, and you will have to write the same prompt as fast as possible
  5. Finally, once the prompt is completed then your statistics will be displayed showing your Score, Accuracy, and WPM


    6. To learn more about the application itself, see embedded gifs, and a demonstration of our application, click on the question mark icon

    To view our code, click on the github icon to visit our repository
